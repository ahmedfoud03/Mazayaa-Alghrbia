    About Us

    Almazzaya Algharbia Factory for the manufacture of knitting bags and self-closing bags It is a modern factory established in 2023 according to the latest methods and machinery found in Yemen and the Middle East To meet the needs of the local market of flour bags, wheat, bran, sugar bags, rice, cement bags and others made of polyproprolene material

    Our Goals
    • Excellence in Quality: Production of woven bags with globally competitive standards, to ensure that the needs and expectations of the market are met.
    • Expansion into New Markets:Strengthen our position in the domestic market and spread to regional and international markets to meet growing demand.
    • Enhancing Environmental Sustainability:Develop environmentally friendly production processes to reduce negative environmental impacts and promote sustainability.
    • Innovation and Renewal:Continuous investment in the latest technologies and the development of innovative products in line with the requirements of global markets.
    • Building Long-Term Partnerships:Create solid and reliable relationships with customers and distributors by providing premium services and products that exceed their expectations.
    Vision

    To become the leading and outstanding company in the woven bag industry in Yemen and the region, by providing innovative, high-quality, reliable packaging and packaging solutions that contribute to the needs of multiple sectors and promote environmental sustainability.

    Mission

    In our factory we commit to manufacture woven bags with the highest quality and safety standards, to meet the needs of various industries, from cement to food etc. We seek to develop durable, eco-friendly, custom-designed packaging solutions that meet our customers' expectations and reflect our value in quality and innovation.
